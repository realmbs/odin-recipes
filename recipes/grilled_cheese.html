Grilled Cheese

Description

The classic and simple grilled cheese sandwich can be reimagined multiple ways just by changing up the bread and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces sliced melting cheese
  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Sandwich 2 slices of cheese between 2 slices of bread.
  2. Preheat a skillet over medium low heat.
  3. Spread or brush the outside of the sandwich with 1 tablespoon of butter.
  4. Cook until lightly browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Flip the sandwich and repeat on other side.
